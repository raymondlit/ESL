参考范文：

The chart illustrates global attitudes toward AI adoption from 2020 to 2025 across three regions. China shows remarkable acceptance growth (75% to 89%), while the US exhibits modest increase (68% to 72%) and the EU experiences significant decline (62% to 51%).

This divergence stems from multiple factors. China's surge relates to government initiatives like the "AI+" strategy and digital infrastructure development. Conversely, European skepticism reflects privacy concerns under GDPR and job displacement fears. American attitudes remain cautiously optimistic, balancing innovation potential with ethical considerations.

In my view, while AI promises efficiency gains in healthcare and transportation, global cooperation is essential to establish ethical frameworks. The upcoming UN AI Ethics Summit should prioritize transnational standards to ensure responsible development.

The 24 Solar Terms represent the crystallization of wisdom from ancient Chinese agricultural civilization, dividing seasons through solar observations. They not only guide agricultural production but also influence dietary customs (e.g., consuming dumplings during Winter Solstice).
